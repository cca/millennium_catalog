Basic Search

Search the CCA New Materials Library.

Select search type, enter search terms, then use the 'enter' key or click the 'find' button.

Search tips

    Keyword: retrieves words in the material name, manufacturer, material terms and details fields, in any order. You may use the Boolean operators AND, OR and NOT. You may also truncate words with an asterisk.
  • Nylon and fabric
  • Porcelain or ceramic
  • Wood and not pine
  • (porcelain or ceramic) and (water* or weather*)
    Company: type the manufacturer's name.
  • Ecotile
  • Ruckstuhl Carpet
    Material Name: type as much or as little of the name as you want.
  • Kev
  • Kevlar
  • Acrylite Acrylic
    Material Terms: type in an exact CCA materials term.
  • glass
  • flooring
  • upholstery
  • polyethylene
  • plywood
  • fabric
  • low VOC
    Sample ID #: type the New Materials Library ID #, starting with the first numbers. You may type the first part of the number only, if you wish to browse by ID. Replace decimal points with a space.
  • 01a 004
  • 01a

Please Note: ID # designates the location of the material sample, and gives a sequential sample number. The materials library displays most samples on 24 vertical panels. Each panel is numbered, and its sides are designated "a" and "b". Each sample ID# begins with the panel location (e.g. 18a), then a decimal point, followed by a sequential sample number. Therefore the first sample on side a of panel 18 is numbered 18a.001.
