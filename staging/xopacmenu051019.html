SEARCH TIPS AND EXAMPLES:

AUTHOR: type the author's or artist's last name, then first name if needed.
For example:

  • Foucault
  • Foucault, Michel
  • Gursky
  • Gursky, Andreas
  • CCA Wattis Institute

TITLE: type as much or as little of the title as you want,
starting with the first words, but ignoring initial articles like "the".
For example:

  • Cultural Studies Reader
  • Cultural Studies R
  • Cultural

KEYWORD: retrieves words in the title, author, subject, and note fields, in any order.
You may use the Boolean operators AND, OR and NOT.
You may also "truncate" words with an asterisk.
For example:

  • Soviet and Russian
  • Soviet Union or Russia
  • Video Art not New
  • (painting or drawing) and (paint* or draw*)

LC SUBJECT: type in an exact Library of Congress Subject Heading,
beginning with the first words, but exclude punctuation.
For example:

  • African American Art 20th Century
  • African American Art
  • Video Art Exhibitions
  • Video Art

CALL NUMBER: type the Library of Congress classification number,
starting with the first letter and numbers.
For example:

  • N 66 V58 1988
  • N 66 V58
  • TS 171 M53 1999
  • TS 171 M
