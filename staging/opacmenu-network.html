Basic search

Look for books, periodicals, and videos in the CCA Libraries.

Select search type, enter search terms, then use the 'enter' key or click the 'find' button.

Search tips

    Keyword: retrieves words in the title, author, subject, and note fields, in any order. You may use the Boolean operators AND, OR and NOT. You may also truncate words with an asterisk.
  • Soviet and Russian
  • Soviet Union or Russia
  • Video Art and not New
  • (painting or drawing) and (paint* or draw*)
    Author: type the author's last name, then first name if needed. Artists are sometimes considered the authors of books containing their works.
  • Foucault
  • Foucault, Michel
  • Gursky
  • Gursky, Andreas
  • CCA Wattis Institute
    Title: type as much or as little of the title phrase as you want, starting with the first words, but ignoring initial articles like "the".
  • Cultural Studies Reader
  • Cultural Studies R
  • Cultural
    Subject: type in an exact Library of Congress Subject Heading, beginning with the first words, but exclude punctuation.
  • African American Art 20th Century
  • African American Art
  • Video Art Exhibitions
  • Video Art
    Call Number: type the Library of Congress call number, starting with the first letters and numbers. By typing the first part of the number only, you can browse by subject classifications.
  • N 66 V58 1988
  • N 66 V58
  • TS 171 M53 1999
  • TS 171 M
